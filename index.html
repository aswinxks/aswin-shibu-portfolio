Biography

01

Roots & Foundations

Born on September 12, 2001, in Angamaly, Kerala, Aswin Shibu’s journey began with a curiosity that spanned multiple fields. He attended St. Francis Assisi Public School in Athani for his primary education, later pursuing high school at Holy Family High School and NSS HSS School in Parakadav.

02

The Entrepreneurial Pivot

While originally enrolled in Sree Sankara College to study Tourism and Hospitality, Aswin’s love for innovation and independent creation led him to a bold decision. He chose to drop out and forge his own path. In 2020, at just 19 years old, he founded Master Industries, providing high-quality products and services through dedication and a visionary mindset.

03

Psychology meets Technology

Aswin, often known as "Master" or "Aswin Trybz", is recognized for his unique expertise at the intersection of psychology and technology. He delves deep into understanding the human psyche and how the digital world impacts human behavior. As a coach and consultant, he has guided many towards overcoming obstacles and achieving their high-performance goals.

04

Lifestyle & Philosophy

A strong advocate for ethical technology and conscious living, Aswin follows a vegetarian lifestyle—his favorite being paneer with chili gobi rice. He is a self-learner with a vast collection of books, studying everything from cybersecurity to astronomy. In his free time, he finds precision and speed in his favorite ride, the CBR 1000 RR Fireblade, often reflected in his favorite colors: Black and Violet.

Despite his young age, Aswin continues to push the boundaries of excellence, striving to leave a lasting impact on society through research, writing, and systems innovation.
